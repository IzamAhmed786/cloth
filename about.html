The Cow is a gentle and domesticated animal that is highly useful to humans. It is primarily raised for its milk , which is used to make various dairy products like butter, cheese, and yogurt. Cows are herbivores and have a unique four-chambered stomach that helps them digest grass and other plant materials. In many rural areas, cow dung is used as natural fertilizer and fuel.
